The story

Will Turner has spent more than 25+ years in rooms with leaders — on stage, in boardrooms, in coaching conversations at 7am before the day begins. And in every one of those rooms, he has been doing the same thing: finding the simple human truth inside the unconscious complexity, and using it to move people forward.

He didn't always have a name for it. But a friend called Helen — in London, 15 years ago — looked at him and said: "You are all about simplexity — making complex things simple. It is a real gift."

Two independent coaching sessions later, the same word surfaced again. From different people, different contexts, different countries. The same truth. So he named it. Simplexity.

And then something even more important happened. He identified the enemy: unconscious complexity — the complexity leaders create beyond what the situation actually requires, without knowing they're doing it. And he built the framework to show them where it lives — and how to move through the penny drop to the other side.

About Will Turner

Will Turner is a keynote speaker, executive coach, and trusted advisor based in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. He is the creator of Simplexity — a discipline for finding the simple human truth inside any complex challenge — and the originator of the concept of unconscious complexity, which he defines as the complexity leaders create beyond what the situation actually requires, without knowing they are doing it.

He has spent more than 25+ years working with leaders across every major industry: financial services, healthcare, technology, retail, professional services, government, education, and not-for-profit. His clients have included ASX-listed companies, global professional services firms, large public sector organisations, and fast-growth private businesses across Australia and Asia-Pacific. He holds an MBA with Distinction from London Business School (2007).

Will's next book, Simplexity: The Simple Truth Inside Everything Complex, is forthcoming in 2026. It sets out the full Simplexity framework — including the Simplexity Matrix, the nature of unconscious complexity, and the method for engineering the penny drop — for leaders, teams, and organisations.
