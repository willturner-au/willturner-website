Simplexity

Why smart leaders make it harder than it needs to be — and how to stop

Most leaders know something is wrong. The weight is real — the meetings that multiply, the strategies that collapse, the decisions that take too long. What they can't name is why. This talk names it.

Will Turner shows leadership audiences precisely how they're creating complexity beyond what the situation requires — without knowing they're doing it. The penny drop happens live. People don't just leave thinking differently. They leave lighter. That's not inspiration. That's Simplexity.

The Fog Index

Measuring what your complexity is actually costing you

Most organisations don't have a complexity problem — they have a measurement problem. They can't see what the complexity is costing them, so they keep creating more of it.
