The central idea

There is a reason everything feels harder than it should. We are all creating complexity beyond what our situations require — in our organisations, our relationships, our thinking, our lives. We just don't know we're doing it.

Will Turner calls this unconscious complexity. And he has spent 25+ years finding the simple human truth inside it — creating the penny drop moment that changes everything.

Simplexity is the book that names this pattern, explains why it happens, and gives readers the tools to move from unconscious complexity to conscious simplicity — and to leave every situation knowing exactly what to do next.

This book applies everywhere. The clarity method that helps a CEO cut through a strategic impasse helps a parent navigate a difficult conversation. Simplexity is a human book. That it transforms leadership too is almost a side effect.
