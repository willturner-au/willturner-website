Will Turner as an executive coach

Will Turner is a Brisbane-based executive coach working with senior leaders — CEOs, founders, C-suite executives, and senior partners — across Australia and internationally. He takes a deliberately small number of clients at any time, typically no more than six to eight, to ensure depth over volume.

His coaching approach is built on a single insight: most leadership challenges are not complexity problems — they are clarity problems dressed as complexity problems. The leader is too close to their own situation to see the simple truth. Will's role is to find that truth and create the penny drop: the moment it becomes visible and impossible to ignore.

Sessions are direct, candid, and free of the language that makes coaching feel like coaching. There are no frameworks to complete, no homework to submit, and no predetermined arc. The relationship lasts as long as it is genuinely useful — and not a day longer.

What makes this different
—Not a programme. No six-week modules, no competency frameworks. Just direct work on the actual challenge the leader is facing right now.
—Not behaviour change. Most coaching focuses on what to do differently. Will focuses on what to see differently — because clarity always comes before action.
—Not a consultant. Will doesn't deliver recommendations. He creates the conditions for the leader to see clearly for themselves — so the answer lands as intuitive certainty, not external advice.
—The penny drop is the output. Every session ends with at least one moment of genuine clarity — one thing the leader now knows that they didn't before.
—Industries. Financial services, professional services, technology, healthcare, retail, government, and not-for-profit. The method is universal.
